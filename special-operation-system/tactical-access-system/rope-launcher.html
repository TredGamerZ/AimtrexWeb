Rope Launcher

Aimtrex offers a pneumatic Rope Launcher designed specifically to enable the user to shoot a grapnel with a rope or a carbon fibre ladder tied behind it to a distance of up to 100 metres. Low weight and short length makes it ideal for land based or amphibious operations.

What is
Rope Launcher ?

The Aimtrex Rope Launcher makes it possible to establish contact with an otherwise inaccessible point, which may be manned or unmanned.Gap can be bridged and the operation then launched. its rugged design makes it operable under extreme weather and terrain conditions; which makes it an ultimate rescue and military grade device. It meets and exceeds the highest safety standards and also, its easy handling provides the user with prompt user familiarisation.

Applications

  • High rise emergency access
  • Search and rescue
  • Fire fighting
  • Cross horizontal obstacles such as rivers/canals
  • Disaster Management
  • Ship to ship and shore to shore line throwing for various marine applications
  • Launch rope lines, climbing ropes, carbon fibre ladders for tactical/rescue operations
  • Launch pilot lines to pull deploy heavier ropes
  • Marine operations
