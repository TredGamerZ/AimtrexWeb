Aimtrex and Uvision, a cutting edge technology company with advanced deployment, target acquisition and attack capabilities, the unique hero line of loitering munitions systems can be utilised by all military units - from the individual soldier to central command - for both strategic and tactical targets. Offering independant, non -LOS precision, Hero's rapid reaction time in response to time-sensitive targets provides it with a critical advantage in populated areas. Hero has consistently demonstrated effectiveness against human targets, moving light-duty vehicles, tanks and other strategic objects - and all with minimal collateral damage.

Uvision has in its fold a number of variants at various stages of developements, our product Hero 30 has already been tested and found fit for induction in a number of Developed countries. Other variants such as Hero - 120 and Hero -400 are in advanced stages of developement.
