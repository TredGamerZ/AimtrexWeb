About AimTrex.

Aimtrex Technologies is an Indian ISO 9001:2008 certified company, dedicated to providing cutting edge solutions to meet the training, operational, and disaster management needs of India’s ArmedForces, Special Forces, Paramilitary Forces, Police Forces, and Disaster – Emergency Response Forces. Our products and services are valued by esteemed organizations across India such as the Indian Army, Navy, Air Force, National Security Guards, Border Security Force.

Why Us?

Simply because we are time tested, leaders of the current trends and have an eye on the future. At Aimtrex, being a ’Nation First Organisation’, we feel and firmly believe that what we offer is not just cutting edge but also comes at a fraction of a cost, in line with our ‘Make in India’ Motto. Our company's philosophy is based on the martial arts principle that if you sweat more in training, you will bleed less in battle.
