What is a
MULTI-FUNCTION TARGET SYSTEM ?

Multi-function is a portable, wireless and battery operated smart target system with an outstanding ability to detect both supersonic and subsonic ammunition at an accuracy of below 3mm. Thus making it the world’s most advance live firing small arms training system.

The movements achieved are pop up and down, rotate, swing out, emerge from behind a cover, moving across a lane, or a combination of movements. The up time, down time and exposure time can be varied as desired. Numerous movement controls can be exercised. The inbuilt illumination enables light conditions experienced by firers. The combined wireless scoring collection enables tracking progress in skills very easy.

The trainee’s reaction time, accuracy in firing, hits on target etc are checked while he has to adapt to field craft use of ground. The system can be effectively be used as Live Firing at all outdoor and indoor shooting range and for training Special Forces, GHATAKS, Commandos, Para Units, Special Task Forces, troops in anti naxal operations, Jungle Lane Shooting Range, Reflex Shooting Range, Kill Houses, Urban / Semi Urban range, Counter Terrorism range, C.Q.B. (close quarter battle) range, Army Regimental Training Centres, Officer Training Academies and Schools.
