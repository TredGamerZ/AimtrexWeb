What is
Hybrid shooting range ?

Small arms firing is presently limited to basic annual range classification. Given the limitations of simulators, it is imperative that this training is conducted on existing outdoor shooting ranges. It is well known that armed forces are facing acute shortages of traditional outdoor ranges due to variety of reasons, which are beyond the immediate control of the centre/units/sub units.

The concept of Hybrid Shooting Ranges has been envisioned with the idea of having a cost effective and time saving design for creating modern shooting ranges which are fully compliant with all safety standards. The design has adopted the best features from indoor shooting ranges and combined them with safety features of a baffle range, to create a hybrid design that caters to the requirement of training the soldiers in conditions akin to battle field environment.
