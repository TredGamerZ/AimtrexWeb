Outdoor Shooting
Ranges

Outdoor Ranges are the most conventional structures designed for training and firing practices. By the nature of design, users can easily and safely customize training drills, targetry and scenarios. outdoor training mimics real life environment since it requires exposure to the elements such as wind, heat or rain.

Tell Me More

What's an
Outdoor Shooting Range ?

Coming in all shapes and sizes, Outdoor ranges can be easily customized as per a user's requirements. Since training drills for snipers, special forces etc. are much different than for basic recruits, a lot of careful planning and execution needs to be conducted for coming up with an Outdoor Range which is not effective but also ensures, delivers and exceeds the global standards for 'Firing Range Safety.'

Also, some room for improvisation also needs to be incorporated in design so that individuals from different commands and specializations can train effectively without any bounds imposed by structural limitations.

Key Features

ALL SMALL FIRE ARMS

Ranges cater to all weapons like 5.56 mm INSAS, 7.62 mm, SLR, 9 mm Carbine, AK-47,MP5, Pistols of all caliber.

NIGHT FIRING CAPABILITY

For changing training scenarios and variable lighting intensity.

THERMAL RECOGNITION SYSTEM

Sophisticated IR sensors which can detect the angle, velocity and accuracy of the rounds fired.

MOBILITY

Multiple movements can be achived: 360 degrees, Pop up, Turning, Slicing, Rotate.

ADVANCED TARGETS

Multiple targets can be controlled by single handheld controller from a range of 1 km.

BULLET TRAP

Aimtrex's patented and customized bullet trap ensures 100000 bullet hits.

Other Great Features

REMOTE OPERATION

All Aimtrex ranges can be controlled Wirelessly from a distance of up to 1 km, Ensuring you are always in control.

SAFETY MEASURES

Special attention to safety provided: firefighting equipment, emergency alarms, emergency exits, and anti-ricochet treatment in every range.

CONTROL STATION

Wireless controller, Ventilation system, Scenario based lighting, CCTV surveillance, PA system, 2 way audio, Scoring, Indexing shooting performance.

HIT DISPLAY

LCD monitors at each firing bay to display location of hits and misses.

UNIQUE STEEL PLATING

The construction is embedded with rugged steel exterior walls made to withstand extreme usage.
