What is
Containerized shooting range ?

Aimtrex Instarange is a next generation solution made in compliance
with world-class standards for live firing shooting ranges.

Some advantages of a containerised shooting ranges are:

  • A flexible alternative to traditional outdoor shooting range
  • An Air conditioned shooting range which can be utilised 24/7, when and where you need it.
  • Easily transportable to any location, can be setup temporarily or permanently.
  • a very cost effective solution made in compliance with world class standards for live firing shooting ranges
