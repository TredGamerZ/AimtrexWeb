What is
Live Fire Simulator ?

In a country like India, trading hundreds or thousands of recruits in a single day is no easy feat for our forces to achieve. To add that to amount of time taken plus the heavy cost of real live fire bullets for shooting is not a very cost effective solution.

Needless to say, the amount of injuries caused by accidents in the forces around the world. Modern technology is trying to make the environment; more competitive and yet much safer in practice. At Aimtrex, being a ’Nation First Organisation’, we feel and firmly believe that what we offer is not just cutting edge but also comes at a fraction of a cost, in line with our ‘Make in India’ Motto.

Live Fire simulation training is a highly interactive platform, where scenario based training can be conducted, a speciality which is not mostly possible with other forms of training. It reduces the risk of injuries and any casualties. The Aimtrex philosophy is to sweat more in training so you may bleed less in battle.

Thermal sensons, motion trackers, virtual reality, vibrational Taser like hits, all combined; make the training more realistic and effective as the brain is more alert and on guard in a gaming environment, a fact science has proven many times over. Call for a demo today to know more
