What is
Live Fire Simulator ?

In a country like India, trading hundreds or thousands of recruits in a single day is no easy feat for our forces to achieve. To add that to amount of time taken plus the heavy cost of real live fire bullets for shooting is not a very cost effective solution. Needless to say, the amount of injuries caused by accidents in the forces around the world. Modern technology is trying to make the environment; more competitive and yet much safer in practice. At Aimtrex, being a ’Nation First Organisation’, we feel and firmly believe that what we offer is not just cutting edge but also comes at a fraction of a cost, in line with our ‘Make in India’ Motto.

Live Fire simulation training is a highly interactive platform, where scenario based training can be conducted, a speciality which is not mostly possible with other forms of training. It reduces the risk of injuries and any casualties. The Aimtrex philosophy is to sweat more in training so you may bleed less in battle.Thermal sensors, motion trackers, virtual reality, vibrational Taser like hits, all combined; make the training more realistic and effective as the brain is more alert and on guard in a gaming environment, a fact science has proven many times over.

The live fire training simulators generate HD video to create a realistic environment. It is available in several configurati︎ons for live firing, and also for the INSTA - RANGE; a highly portable laptop trainer; a classroom-based desktop simulator; a live-fire training system for an indoor range; and it can be fi︎ed inside a live-fire modular INSTA - RANGE container. The advanced features built into the simulation make it a viable tool for sniper training. With accurate external ballisti︎cs modelling, simulated wind, and the ability to combine HD video with animated computer graphics, The simulation can generate highly realis︎ticscenarios for the most demanding training applica︎tions.

The simulation judgemental trainers provide interacti︎ve, branching video scenarios that require trainees to make decisions during potenti︎ally life- threatening situati︎ons. The simulator can capture video of the trainee and the instructor can play back the recording,

giving the trainee immediate feedback on their performance.
