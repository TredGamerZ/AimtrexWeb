What is
CLOSE QUARTER COMBAT SYSTEM?

As the engineer and fabricator of the Digital Modular Shoot House, Aimtrex is able to provide a custom designed close quarters shooting facility to match any agency’s training objectives and budget. As per the training requirements of the organisation, both ballistic and non-ballistic environments can be setup.

Aimtrex CQB systems enable full-immersion experiences with life-sized, complete virtual worlds projected onto the walls of the facility. Each facility has the ability to communicate with other simulations. Breaching options include explosive, pry, ram, and shotgun breaching doors, breaching windows etc.

Key Features


Electronic Target Systems

Software controlled Electronic Target Systems with capability to rotate, pop up and swing all with one machine.

Anti-Ricochet Covering

The surfaces of the range area where targets will be placed and shot need to protect by anti-ricochet material/flooring to prevent the bullets causing damage due to ricochet.

Bullet Traps

The Bullet Trap will be installed at each target end. The bullet trapper should absorb all correctly aimed shots. The area around the Bullet Traps is likely to be hit by the bullet. These areas need to identified and secured with solid rubber bricks that absorb and retain a bullet.

Floors

Floors are constructed out of cement and are ideal for ricochet bullets.

Sound Suppression

Special arrangements for sound suppression is required on the inside of the range.

Fire Hazard Reduction Measures

Any building consisting of such high value equipment has high end firefighting arrangement including audio alarm system.
